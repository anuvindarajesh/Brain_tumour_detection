Features

This project develops an efficient system for brain tumour detection using image segmentation. It leverages deep learning and MRI data for accurate localization, quantitative analysis, and intuitive visualization, aiding early diagnosis and treatment planning.

Accurate Tumour Localization

Utilizes advanced image segmentation techniques to precisely identify and isolate tumour regions in brain MRI scans.

Quantitative Analysis

Provides measurements such as tumour volume and size, aiding in disease assessment and treatment planning.

Enhanced Visualization

Generates color-coded overlays on MRI scans for easy and intuitive visualization of tumour areas.
